Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

Unami Foundation Moments is built on trust. We collect minimal data, never sell your information, and give you full control over your participation.

What We Collect

  • WhatsApp Phone Number: To send you moments and process your commands
  • Province Preferences: To send relevant local content
  • Messages You Send: Content you share for potential broadcast
  • Subscription Status: Whether you're opted in or out

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your name or personal identity
  • Your location beyond province selection
  • Your contacts or WhatsApp profile
  • Individual reading or engagement tracking
  • Browsing history or device information

How We Use Your Data

  • Message Delivery: Send moments to your WhatsApp based on your province preferences
  • Content Moderation: Review submitted content for safety and relevance
  • Aggregate Analytics: Track total subscribers and broadcasts (not individual behavior)
  • Service Improvement: Understand what content serves communities best

Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share information when:

  • Required by South African law
  • Necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity
  • You explicitly consent to sharing

Your Rights (POPIA Compliance)

Under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you
  • Correction: Update your province preferences anytime
  • Deletion: Send "STOP" to unsubscribe and delete your data
  • Objection: Opt out of any data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a readable format

Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • Encrypted database storage (Supabase)
  • Secure WhatsApp Business API
  • Access controls limiting who can view data
  • Regular security audits

Data Retention

  • Active Subscribers: Data retained while you're subscribed
  • Unsubscribed Users: Data deleted within 30 days of "STOP" command
  • Broadcast Logs: Aggregate stats retained for 12 months

Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services:

  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta): Message delivery
  • Supabase: Database and storage

Each service has its own privacy policy. We choose partners committed to data protection.

Children's Privacy

Moments is designed for adults 18+. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify subscribers of major privacy policy changes via WhatsApp broadcast.
